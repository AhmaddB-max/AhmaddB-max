Mana Shamim Company was founded in 2021 through the collaboration of experienced and specialized managers, with a vision to redefine quality and innovation in the cosmetic and hygiene industry. From the outset, the company has been committed to producing a diverse range of products that meet the highest global standards. Situated in the Eshtehard Industrial Zone, Mana Shamim operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility equipped with multiple production lines, enabling it to deliver premium-quality products at scale. This strategic location not only ensures seamless operations but also allows the company to serve both domestic and international markets efficiently.

The company boasts a broad and diverse portfolio of products that cater to various customer needs. These include air fresheners that create refreshing and aromatic environments, body sprays designed for personal care and comfort, hair hygiene sprays to support scalp health, and high-quality colognes to complete the grooming experience. By focusing on innovation and variety, Mana Shamim has positioned itself as a leader in the industry, meeting the dynamic preferences of its customers. These products are crafted with a meticulous approach, ensuring they stand out in terms of quality, design, and performance.


A significant aspect of Mana Shamim’s vision is to support local markets while simultaneously expanding its footprint beyond borders. The company actively addresses domestic needs by offering high-quality products at competitive prices, thereby reducing dependency on imports. Moreover, its focus on export markets, particularly in neighboring countries, reflects its ambition to establish itself as a prominent regional player. By leveraging its strategic location and robust production capabilities, Mana Shamim has successfully created a niche for itself in the competitive global marketplace.

The company's success is rooted in its unwavering commitment to excellence in every step of the production process. Mana Shamim uses high-grade raw materials sourced from trusted suppliers in Iran, Turkey, and Spain to ensure consistent product quality. It also relies on a team of highly skilled and experienced professionals who bring expertise and innovation to the table. By adhering to strict managerial and operational standards, the company has been able to achieve remarkable growth in a relatively short span of time. These principles have enabled Mana Shamim to maintain a competitive edge while fostering a culture of innovation and reliability.

To date, Mana Shamim has set an impressive production record of manufacturing 1.5 million sprays per month, a milestone that underscores its operational efficiency and market demand. The company is constantly evolving, with ongoing efforts to expand its production capacity and develop new product lines. By combining innovation, sustainability, and a commitment to quality, Mana Shamim aims to further solidify its position as a trusted name in the cosmetic and hygiene industry. The company’s journey is a testament to its vision of delivering excellence while contributing to the growth of the regional and global markets.
