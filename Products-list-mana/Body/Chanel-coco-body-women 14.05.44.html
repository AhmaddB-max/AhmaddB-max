How to Use:

To use the **Royal Coco Mademoiselle Women's Body Spray**, simply spray it onto clean and dry skin, especially on pulse points of the body:

  • Behind the ears
  • Wrists
  • Underarms
  • Back of the neck

These points, due to the natural warmth of the body, help the fragrance to diffuse evenly and last throughout the day. You can also spray it on your clothes and hair to enjoy a soft and long-lasting scent all day.

For enhanced fragrance longevity, you can also apply the spray on your clothes or hair a few moments before getting dressed.

Note: Spray from a distance of about 15 cm onto your body to ensure the fragrance is evenly distributed and lasts longer.
