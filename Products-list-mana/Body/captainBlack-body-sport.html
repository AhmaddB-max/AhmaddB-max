How to Use:

To use the Royal Captain Black Sport Body Spray , simply spray it on clean and dry skin. The best areas to apply the spray are:

Note: Hold the spray about 10 to 15 centimeters away from your skin or clothing to ensure an even and lasting fragrance application.
