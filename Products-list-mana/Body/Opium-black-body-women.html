A breif History

The Royal Black Opium Women's Body Spray is a product from the luxurious French brand Yves Saint Laurent. Known as a pioneer in the world of fashion and fragrance, Yves Saint Laurent has always been a symbol of charm, allure, and style. One of its most iconic fragrances, Opium, was first introduced in 1977, captivating women and men alike with its seductive and powerful aroma.

Later, Black Opium was introduced in 2009 as a newer, more energetic version of the classic scent, featuring a bolder and darker fragrance profile. Its smoky and oriental notes made it particularly appealing to women who prefer strong and sophisticated perfumes. Royal Black Opium Women's Body Spray, inspired by this luxurious classic, is designed for those who desire a lasting and unique fragrance, making it an excellent choice.

Fragrance Notes:

The Royal Black Opium Women's Body Spray features a bold and seductive scent, built on oriental and smoky notes. This combination creates an experience of warmth, pleasure, and allure for the wearer.

Top Notes: Pink pepper and pear, providing a spicy, fruity, and captivating aroma. These notes particularly enhance a sense of bravery and confidence.

Base Notes: Incense, sandalwood, vanilla, and musk. These base notes give the spray depth and longevity, creating a warm and smoky fragrance that lingers throughout the day.

How to Use:

For the best results with the Opium Black Royal Body Spray for Women ,apply it to clean and dry skin, especially on areas more prone to perspiration:

  • Behind the ears
  • Wrists
  • Underarms
  • Back of the neck

These areas of the body, due to their higher temperature and natural warmth, are the best spots for better fragrance diffusion. Spraying on these points ensures the scent is evenly and long-lastingly dispersed.

For enhanced fragrance longevity, you can also apply the spray on your clothes or hair a few moments before getting dressed.

Note: Spray from a distance of about 15 cm onto your body to ensure the fragrance is evenly distributed and lasts longer.
