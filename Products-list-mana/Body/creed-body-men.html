A breif History

The Royal Creed Aventus Men's Body Spray is a version of the incredibly famous and luxurious Aventus fragrance by the brand Creed, introduced in 2010. This perfume quickly became one of the best-selling and most popular men's fragrances in the history of perfumery, gaining fans across the globe.

Aventus, crafted by Olivier Creed and Erwin Creed, was initially designed as an exclusive fragrance to celebrate grand occasions. Its scent is inspired by historical figures such as Napoleon Bonaparte, symbolizing triumphs in battle and achieving monumental success. Aventus is celebrated as a symbol of power, determination, and victory.

The Royal Creed Aventus Men's Body Spray is a version of this beloved fragrance, designed in a convenient spray form for daily use. This spray features a rich, sophisticated, and refreshing scent, crafted especially for men with strong personalities and high self-confidence.

Fragrance Notes:

The Royal Creed Aventus Men's Body Spray features a blend of fruity, woody, and smoky notes, creating a unique and captivating aroma. This spray seamlessly combines a sense of power and freedom with freshness and allure.

Top Notes: Pineapple, green apple, blackcurrant, and bergamot. These notes bring freshness and vitality to the spray, delivering an invigorating and energetic experience right from the start.

Heart Notes: Rose, jasmine, geranium, and birch. These notes emerge shortly after application, adding a pleasant and delightful scent to the spray while enhancing its complexity.

Base Notes: Leather, musk, amber, cedarwood, and vanilla. These warm, woody, and smoky notes form the foundation of the spray, providing exceptional longevity and depth.

How to Use:

To use the Royal Creed Aventus Men's Body Spray, simply spray it onto clean and dry skin. The best areas to apply the spray for optimal fragrance diffusion are the pulse points of your body, which include:

  • Behind the ears
  • Wrists
  • Underarms
  • Back of the neck

These areas are ideal due to the natural warmth of the body, which enhances the even and long-lasting diffusion of the fragrance throughout the day.

Note: Spray from a distance of about 10 to 15 cm onto your skin or clothing for an even and enduring scent.
