How to Use:

To use the Royal 540 Baccarat Rouge Sport Body Spray , simply spray it on clean and dry skin. The best areas to apply the spray are:

These areas are ideal for fragrance diffusion due to the natural warmth of the body. Spraying on these points ensures an even and long-lasting scent throughout the day.

Note: Hold the spray about 10 to 15 centimeters away from your skin or clothing to ensure an even and lasting fragrance application.
