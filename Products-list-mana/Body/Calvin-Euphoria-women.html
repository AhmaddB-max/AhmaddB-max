A breif History

The Royal Calvin Klein Euphoria Women's Body Spray is part of the Euphoria fragrance collection produced by the renowned brand Calvin Klein. Established in 1968, Calvin Klein has grown over the decades to become one of the leading names in fashion, apparel, and fragrance globally. The Euphoria perfume, introduced in 2005, quickly became one of the most popular fragrances worldwide. Following its immense success, Calvin Klein decided to create various versions of the fragrance, including the Royal Euphoria Women's Body Spray.

The Royal Calvin Klein Euphoria Women's Body Spray is specifically designed for women seeking a distinctive, long-lasting fragrance suitable for daily use. This spray offers a luxurious and delightful scent that perfectly complements the experience of the Euphoria perfume, bringing freshness and elegance to your body.

Fragrance Notes:

The Royal Calvin Klein Euphoria Women's Body Spray features a seductive, floral, and fruity aroma that beautifully conveys femininity and allure. The blend of various notes ensures the fragrance lasts throughout the day, providing freshness and confidence.

Top Notes: Pomegranate, mandarin, and freesia. These notes create a fresh and invigorating start with cool, fruity scents.

Heart Notes: Black orchid, iris, and lotus blossom. These floral notes add a soft and feminine touch to the fragrance.

Base Notes: Amber, rosewood, musk, and vanilla. These notes provide depth and longevity, making the scent a complex and delightful experience.

How to Use:

For the best results with the Royal Calvin Klein Euphoria Women's Body Spray,apply it to clean and dry skin, especially on areas more prone to perspiration:

  • Behind the ears
  • Wrists
  • Underarms
  • Back of the neck

These areas of the body, due to their higher temperature and natural warmth, are the best spots for better fragrance diffusion. Spraying on these points ensures the scent is evenly and long-lastingly dispersed.

For enhanced fragrance longevity, you can also apply the spray on your clothes or hair a few moments before getting dressed.

Note: Spray from a distance of about 15 cm onto your body to ensure the fragrance is evenly distributed and lasts longer.
