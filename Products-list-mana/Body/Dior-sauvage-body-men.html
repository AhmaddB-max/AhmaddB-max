How to Use:

To use the Royal Dior Sauvage Men's Body Spray, simply spray it onto clean and dry skin. The best areas to apply the spray for optimal fragrance diffusion are the pulse points of your body, including:

  • Behind the ears
  • Wrists
  • Underarms
  • Back of the neck

These areas are ideal due to the natural warmth of the body, which enhances even and long-lasting diffusion of the fragrance throughout the day.

Note: Spray from a distance of about 10 to 15 cm onto your skin or clothing for an even and enduring scent.
