Fragrance Notes

The fragrance of Zara Sublime Epoque Woman Royal is both complex and delicate, specifically designed for women who enjoy perfumes with floral and woody notes. The combination of scents conveys both elegance and strength simultaneously.

  • Top Notes: The fragrance begins with notes of pear, pink pepper, and orange blossom, which give a fresh and energizing start to the perfume. These citrusy notes provide a refreshing and lively opening.
  • Middle Notes: After some time, the scents of coffee and jasmine emerge, adding a delicate and feminine touch. This floral combination leads the perfume towards a smoother and more complex scent, enhancing its freshness and beauty.
  • Base Notes: Finally, vanilla, cedarwood, and patchouli in the base add depth and longevity to the perfume. These woody and earthy notes make the fragrance heavier and more serious, while also imparting a luxurious feeling that lasts throughout the day.

This unique combination of floral, woody, and citrus notes makes Zara Sublime Epoque Woman a very special and delightful fragrance, perfect for special and formal occasions as well as for everyday wear.

Best times of consumption
  • Autumn and Winter: In these colder seasons, Zara Sublime Epoque can be used as a chic and luxurious fragrance for evening parties, formal events, and special meetings. The woody and musk notes in these seasons create warmth and grandeur, easily permeating the cold environment.
  • Spring and Summer: In the spring, this perfume can also be worn, especially on cooler and milder days. However, in summer, when lighter perfumes are preferred, this scent might feel slightly heavier. On spring days or summer nights with milder weather, this fragrance will be an appealing choice.
