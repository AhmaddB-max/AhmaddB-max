Fragrance Notes

The fragrance of Zara Amber Fusion for Women is an amazing combination of warm and sweet notes with hints of floral and woody touches. This captivating perfume consists of various layers that convey a sense of luxury and allure throughout the day.

  • Top Notes: Initially, the scents of mandarin orange, bergamot, and marine notes are detected, giving the fragrance a special freshness and vitality. This citrusy and floral combination creates a bright and refreshing start to the perfume.
  • Middle Notes: In the middle, the notes of red fruits and iris emerge, adding a beautiful and delicate femininity to the fragrance. These floral notes, along with hints of orange blossom, bring a complex and refined scent that suits any occasion.
  • Base Notes: Finally, amber and woody notes in the base provide depth and lasting power. These warm and woody notes make Zara Amber Fusion a unique and long-lasting fragrance that lingers throughout the day, transferring a sense of luxury to you.

This amazing combination of floral, woody, and warm notes makes Zara Amber Fusion a unique fragrance, perfect for women with complex and sophisticated tastes.

Best times of consumption
  • Autumn and Winter: During these cold seasons, the amber and sandalwood notes perform best, creating a luxurious and warming sensation in the cold air. This perfume is ideal for cold nights and formal events, and due to its long-lasting nature, it stays throughout the night.
  • Spring and Summer: Although this perfume is designed specifically for the colder seasons, it can also be a good choice on mild spring days. In summer, and on very hot days, the sweet and warm notes of this fragrance might feel slightly heavy, so it might be better to use lighter perfumes during extremely hot weather.
