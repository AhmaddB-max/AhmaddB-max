Fragrance Notes

Royal's Zara ROSE TEMERAIRE has a floral, sweet, and refreshing scent that makes it perfect for the spring and summer seasons. With the use of rose and fruit notes, it brings a sense of delicacy and freshness, while the woody base notes add depth and longevity to the fragrance.

  • Top Notes: This fragrance starts with a blend of sandalwood, tobacco, coffee, and patchouli, bringing a burst of freshness and positive energy. These notes convey a sense of freshness and make the perfume perfect for the warm days of the year.
  • Middle Notes: In this stage, the scents of lily of the valley, white jasmine, and iris root come to the forefront, providing a sweet and pleasant fragrance. This floral combination turns the perfume into a soft and feminine sensory experience.
  • Base Notes: In the base, a combination of tonka bean, cocoa pod peel, vanilla, and sandalwood gives depth and longevity to this perfume. These notes create a sense of warmth and tranquility, ensuring that the fragrance lingers longer on the skin.
Best times of consumption
  • Spring and Summer: The fruity and floral notes of this perfume make it an ideal choice for spring and summer days. The freshness and vitality of citrus and rose notes provide energy and refreshment on warm and sunny days.
  • Autumn and Winter: With the woody notes in the base, this fragrance can also be suitable for the colder seasons. The sandalwood and musk notes add depth and warmth, making it a great choice for cool and cold days.
