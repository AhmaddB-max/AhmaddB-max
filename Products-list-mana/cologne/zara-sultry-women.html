Fragrance Notes

The fragrance of

Zara Sultry Pear Woman is designed with a blend of fruity, floral, and woody notes that bring a sense of freshness, delicacy, and warmth. The combination of fragrances in this perfume creates a gentle and captivating scent at every moment.

  • Top Notes: Initially, citrusy and fruity scents like lemon, orange, and pear emerge, creating a special freshness and vitality. These notes convey a sense of lightness and joy.
  • Middle Notes: As it develops, floral and green notes gradually appear. The scent of jasmine and almond gives this composition a softness and unique allure.
  • Base Notes: Finally, the prominent notes of vanilla and praline provide depth and lasting power to the perfume, creating a feeling of warmth and comfort.
Best times of consumption
  • Spring and Summer: The initial citrus and fruity notes of Zara Sultry Pear make it an excellent choice for the warm days of spring and summer. These scents bring freshness and energy to sunny days, creating a unique sense of freshness.
  • Autumn and Winter: With the appearance of woody and vanilla notes in the base, this perfume harmonizes well with the colder seasons. The warm and rich scents in these seasons create a sense of comfort and luxury, making it a great choice for the colder days of the year.
