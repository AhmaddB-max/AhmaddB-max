Fragrance Notes

The fragrance of Zara Golden Decade Woman Royal is a blend of floral, sweet, and woody notes, making it a delightful and appealing choice. This perfume starts with gentle and sweet notes and gradually transitions to more complex and deeper layers.

  • Top Notes: Initially, scents of black currant, mandarin orange, and neroli leaves emerge, creating a sense of freshness and vitality. These top notes are refreshing and gentle, blending beautifully with the other layers.
  • Middle Notes: Over time, floral notes of lavender, jasmine, and orange blossom appear. These floral notes add a unique softness and beauty to the fragrance, enhancing its feminine feel. At this stage, the perfume gradually develops more sweetness, conveying a sense of comfort and delight.
  • Base Notes: Finally, notes of vanilla, amber, musk, and cedarwood emerge in the base. These woody and sweet notes provide the fragrance with depth and longevity, delivering a warm and luxurious feeling.

This combination of floral, fruity, and woody notes makes Zara Golden Decade a perfectly balanced and captivating fragrance that simultaneously conveys a sense of freshness, elegance, and strength.
