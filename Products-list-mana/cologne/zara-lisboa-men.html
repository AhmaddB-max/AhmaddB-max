Fragrance Notes

Lisboa for Men Royal features a unique, chic, and natural fragrance with a blend of woody, herbal, and spicy notes. Let's explore the main notes of this perfume:

  • Top notes: Lemon, Bergamot, Violet leaf, and Mint
  • Middle notes: Lavender, Cinnamon, Geranium, and Leather
  • Base notes: Amber, Musk, and Frankincense

The combination of top notes, made up of fresh and cool citrus such as lemon and bergamot, gives an immediate feeling of freshness and energy. Over time, the middle notes, including lavender, cinnamon, and geranium, impart a herbal and slightly spicy aroma to the fragrance. Finally, with woody and musky base notes, the perfume offers high longevity and imparts a sense of power and tranquility.
