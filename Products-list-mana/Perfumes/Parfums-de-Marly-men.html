A breif History

**Parfums de Marly** is a French fragrance brand established in 2009, which quickly gained a special place in the world of luxury perfumery. Inspired by the French royal court of the 18th century, the brand creates perfumes with unique blends and exceptionally high quality.

**Pegasus**, first introduced in 2011, quickly became one of the signature fragrances of the brand and gained popularity among luxury men's fragrance enthusiasts. Following the success of the original version, **Pegasus Royal** was introduced in 2024 as a more exclusive and luxurious edition of the collection. This version, with its more complex scent and new design, is the ideal choice for those seeking a remarkable experience with royal and elegant fragrances.
