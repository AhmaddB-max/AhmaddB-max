A breif History

**Amouage**, established in Oman in 1983, has become one of the most prestigious brands in the world of perfumery. The fragrance **Interlude**, introduced in 2007, quickly became one of the brand's iconic scents. With its blend of woody, smoky, and spicy notes, this perfume conveys a sense of complexity and grandeur to its wearers.

Fragrance Notes

**Interlude Royal for Men**, with its rich and complex aroma, is designed for men with distinctive and remarkable personalities. The composition of its notes is crafted to simultaneously embody a sense of power, courage, and elegance:

  • Top Notes: Pepper, Thyme, Amber
  • Middle Notes: Jasmine, Benzoin Resin, Labdanum
  • Base Notes: Leather, Sandalwood, Oud, Patchouli

The opening notes of **Interlude Royal** with **pepper** and **thyme** deliver a burst of energy and courage, while the heart notes of **amber** and **benzoin resin** add warmth and complexity to the blend. The woody and smoky base notes of **leather**, **sandalwood**, and **oud** create a deep, regal, and enduring experience. Finally, **patchouli** in the base adds a touch of sweetness and distinctive warmth to complete the fragrance.

This unique combination of notes is not only captivating but also highly suitable for those seeking a fragrance with depth and complexity. **Interlude Royal** has a rich and intense aroma, making it ideal for special evenings and formal occasions.

Season Suitability

**Interlude Royal for Men**, with its complex and rich composition, is best suited for **autumn and winter**. During these seasons, the warm, woody, and smoky notes of this fragrance stand out beautifully, providing a sense of warmth and comfort in the cold weather. It is an excellent companion for cool autumn and winter evenings. In spring and summer, the intensity of this perfume might feel a bit overwhelming, so it is recommended to avoid using it during these warmer seasons.
