A breif History

**Thierry Mugler** is a renowned and luxurious French brand that has made a significant name in the fields of fashion and perfumery. Over recent decades, the **Mugler** brand has gained a large following by producing iconic perfumes such as **Alien**, **Ambrescent**, and **Couturissime**. **Alien** was first launched in 2005 and quickly became one of the symbols of women’s fragrances. Since then, various versions of this perfume have been introduced, each with its unique features, including **Alien Royal**, which was released in 2024 as a more luxurious and exclusive edition of the collection.
