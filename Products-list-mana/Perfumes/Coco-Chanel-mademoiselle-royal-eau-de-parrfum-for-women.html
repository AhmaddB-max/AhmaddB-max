A breif History

**Coco Mademoiselle** was introduced by the **Chanel** brand in 2001 and quickly became one of the most popular women's fragrances. This perfume, with its cool and refreshing scent, combined floral and citrus notes to bring youthfulness, vitality, and allure to women. Later, a more luxurious and distinctive version, named **Coco Mademoiselle Royal**, was launched, becoming a true gem in the world of fragrances.

Fragrance Notes

**Coco Chanel Mademoiselle Royal for Women** combines floral, citrus, and woody notes beautifully to create an elegant, sophisticated, and at the same time, gentle fragrance. Here are the main notes of this perfume:

These scents harmonize beautifully, enhancing a sense of calm, confidence, and allure.
