A breif History

The **Opium** perfume was first introduced to the fragrance world in 1977 by **Yves Saint Laurent** and quickly became one of the most well-known and best-selling women’s fragrances. **Opium**, with its rich, oriental scent, was designed for women seeking a distinctive and unique fragrance. Later, **Opium Black Royal** was launched as a special and luxurious version of this perfume. This new version, with a focus on the complexity and richness of its notes, offered fragrance enthusiasts a fresh experience of Opium.
